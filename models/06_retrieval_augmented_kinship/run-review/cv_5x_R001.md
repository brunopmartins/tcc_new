# Model 06 — 5-fold Cross-Validation of R001

**Period:** 2026-06-01 → 2026-06-03 (≈35 h GPU wall-clock, AMD RX 6750 XT)
**Hardware:** ROCm 5.7, single GPU, batch 8 × grad-accum 4 (eff. 32).
**Folds:** family-disjoint 5-fold over RFIW Track-I `train-pairs.csv`
families; official RFIW Track-I test set (13,425 pairs) preserved
unchanged across folds. Splitter:
[shared/dataset.py `create_fiw_5fold_train_val_loaders`](../../shared/dataset.py).
Runner: [AMD/cv_runner_r001.sh](../AMD/cv_runner_r001.sh). Output: `output/003/fold_{0..4}/`.

## Motivation

R001 (Test AUC 0.776 single-run) is the retrieval-augmented baseline in
the TCC comparison. Like B0, M02, and M12, it needs a CV band so the
comparison table reports mean ± std under a uniform methodology rather
than a single favorable/unfavorable draw.

| Knob | Value |

## Single-run vs CV mean

| Metric | R001 single-run | CV mean (n=5) | Δ (single − CV) | σ-distance |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Test ROC AUC | 0.7763 | 0.7776 ± 0.0079 | −0.0013 | −0.16σ |

The single-run number (0.776) sits essentially on the CV mean (0.7776),
−0.16σ away. R001 was a representative draw, not a lucky or unlucky one.
σ_AUC = 0.0079 is about twice the M02/M12 fold variance (~0.004),
consistent with the extra variance the retrieval gallery introduces
across family-disjoint splits.

M06 R001 is the **lowest-AUC trained model** in the project — it even
sits below the no-training B0 baseline by 0.0215 AUC. Paired t-test
(see [cv_statistical.md](../../../cv_statistical.md)) confirms every
other model beats M06 at p < 0.05.

## Why M06 underperforms

The retrieval-augmented design was a negative result (documented in
RUN_LOG.md): the gallery of positive-only supports + strong
cross-attention encourages the model to memorise gallery↔val
correlations that do not transfer to the family-disjoint test set. The
CV confirms this is structural, not a single bad draw — all 5 folds
land in the 0.764–0.784 band, below B0.

## Implications for the TCC narrative

1. **Quote M06 R001 as CV mean 0.7776 ± 0.0079** in comparison tables.
   The single-run 0.776 is a faithful representative.
2. **M06 below B0 is a real, reproducible finding.** A trained
   retrieval-augmented model failing to beat a frozen embedding is the
   clearest "this architecture does not help" result in the project,
   and it holds across all 5 folds.
3. **σ_AUC ≈ 0.008 for M06** is ~2× the M02/M12 noise floor — worth
   noting that the retrieval mechanism adds fold-level variance.
