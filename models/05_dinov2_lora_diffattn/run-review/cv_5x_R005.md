# Model 05 — 5-fold Cross-Validation of R005

**Period:** 2026-06-03 → 2026-06-09 (≈150 h GPU wall-clock, AMD RX 6750 XT —
the slowest model in the project at ~91 min/epoch under full-unfreeze DINOv2).
**Hardware:** ROCm 5.7, single GPU, batch 4 × grad-accum 8 (eff. 32).
**Folds:** family-disjoint 5-fold over RFIW Track-I `train-pairs.csv`
families; official RFIW Track-I test set (13,425 pairs) preserved
unchanged across folds. Splitter:
[shared/dataset.py `create_fiw_5fold_train_val_loaders`](../../shared/dataset.py).
Runner: [AMD/cv_runner_r005.sh](../AMD/cv_runner_r005.sh). Output: `output/010/fold_{0..4}/`.

## Motivation

R005 (Test AUC 0.822 single-run) is the strongest DINOv2-LoRA
configuration (full backbone unfreeze + M02-style LR) and the M05
entry in the TCC comparison. It needs a CV band like the other models
so the comparison table reports mean ± std under a uniform methodology.

## R005 recipe (replicated exactly)

| Knob | Value |
|---|---|
| Backbone | DINOv2 ViT-B/14 (**full fine-tune**, 93.5M trainable) |
| Adaptation | LoRA overlay (r=8, α=16) + differential cross-attention |

| Epochs (max) | 20 |
| Patience | 10 |
| Seed | 42 |

This is the exact configuration of M05 R005 from
[RUN_LOG.md](../RUN_LOG.md) Run 005.

The single-run AUC (0.822) sits +1.13σ above the CV mean (0.8144) —
a mild favorable draw, consistent with the project-wide pattern where
single runs landed on the upper side of the distribution. σ_AUC =
0.0067 is between the M02/M12 floor (~0.004) and the M06 variance
(~0.008), as expected for a full-unfreeze model with more degrees of
freedom across folds.

M05 R005 sits **above B0 and M06 but clearly below M02 R031** (−0.0318
AUC). The full-unfreeze DINOv2 beats both the no-training baseline and
the retrieval-augmented model, but does not reach the supervised ViT
+ FaCoR of M02, and is ~0.06 AUC below the M12 family. The ranking
matches the single-run picture.

## Why M05 caps below M02/M12

The CV confirms the single-run conclusion: DINOv2 (general
self-supervised features) + LoRA + full unfreeze tops out around
0.81-0.82 AUC, structurally below the identity-aware AdaFace IR-101
backbone of M12 and even below the ImageNet ViT + FaCoR of M02. The
val→test gap of ≈ -0.07 is stable across all 5 folds, indicating the
gap is a property of how the DINOv2 features transfer across the
family-disjoint split, not a single bad draw. General visual features
are a weaker starting point for kinship than a face-recognition
backbone.

## Implications for the TCC narrative

1. **Quote M05 R005 as CV mean 0.8144 ± 0.0067** in comparison tables.
   The single-run 0.822 was a +1.13σ favorable draw.
2. **M05 above B0/M06, below M02/M12 is reproducible** across all 5
   folds. The "general SSL backbone is not enough for kinship" claim
   holds under CV, not just on a single run.
3. **σ_AUC ≈ 0.0067 for M05** — between the M02/M12 floor (~0.004) and
   M06 (~0.008). Full-unfreeze adds moderate fold-level variance.
